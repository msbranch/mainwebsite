About Morgan

Built by someone who lived the gap.

Morgan Branch

Morgan Branch did not grow up learning about money. She grew up watching what happens when a family works hard but never has access to the right information — the cycles that repeat, the gaps that never get filled, and the wealth that never gets built. That experience did not discourage her. It became her direction.

She is a licensed life insurance agent and a recent MBA graduate with a concentration in Finance from Morgan State University. Over the last two years, she has worked directly with clients to help them understand their financial picture, identify what is missing, and take the first real steps toward building something that lasts. Her approach is straightforward: no jargon, no judgment, and no pretending that money conversations are easy when nobody ever taught you how to have them.

Morgan is a woman of color, a first-generation wealth builder, and a business owner in the early stages of building a practice she intends to scale. She built this work not just as a career, but as a commitment — to herself, to her clients, and to the families that come after them. She believes that generational wealth is not a privilege reserved for people who started with more. It is a structure. And structures can be built.

She started with her own. Now she helps others build theirs.
